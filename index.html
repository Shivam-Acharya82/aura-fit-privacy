AURA-FIT Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

AURA-FIT is a fitness tracking app that helps users manage workouts, progress photos, step/activity tracking, fasting logs, premium access, and fitness analytics.

Privacy, security, and user control

Information We Collect

Depending on the features you use, AURA-FIT may collect and process:

  • Account profile data such as name, email address, age, gender, and Firebase User ID.
  • Authentication data required for email login and Google Sign-In.
  • Step count and activity data read from your device's physical pedometer sensor (to track steps, calculate estimated walking distance, and estimate active calories burned).
  • Workout logs, exercises completed, and training duration records.
  • Meal logs, calorie logs, and macronutrient tracking entries.
  • Fasting logs and timer history.
  • Transformation records, weight entries, progress photos, and memory logs.
  • Google Play Billing subscription status, payment status, and subscription expiry details.
  • Camera/gallery images selected by you for profile photos and memories (uploaded securely via Cloudinary).
  • Notification preferences for workout reminders, fasting timers, and achievements.
  • Aura Points, achievement badges, and fitness milestone records earned within the app.
  • App performance data, crash logs, and diagnostic usage statistics (if analytics are enabled).

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your AURA-FIT account.
  • To sync your data across devices when you sign in with the same account.
  • To show workout progress, step reports, metabolic trends, and analytics.
  • To save progress photos and show transformation history.
  • To calculate activity stats such as estimated distance, calories burned, and workout duration.
  • To calculate Aura Points, achievement milestones, and non-cash in-app rewards for completing fitness goals.
  • To manage premium access and verify purchases.
  • To send reminders and completion notifications when enabled.
  • To improve app reliability, security, and user experience.

Cloud Services We Use

  • Firebase Authentication: used for email login and Google login.
  • Firebase Firestore: used to store profile data, premium status, workout progress, logs, settings, and Cloudinary image URLs.
  • Cloudinary: used to upload and host profile photos, memory photos, and other user-selected images.
  • Google Play Billing: used for premium purchases on Android builds.

AURA-FIT does not store image files directly in Firestore. Firestore stores only the image URL returned by Cloudinary.

Location Data

AURA-FIT does not collect precise location data in this release. Step count, estimated distance, and calories are calculated using the phone pedometer sensor when permission is granted.

Phone Pedometer and Fitness Data

AURA-FIT requests the ACTIVITY_RECOGNITION permission on Android devices to read step data directly from the phone's physical pedometer and step counter sensors. This data is used to calculate daily step counts, estimated walking distance, and active calorie burn totals shown in your progress reports and fitness analytics.

Privacy and Limited Use: This step sensor and activity recognition data is processed entirely locally on your device to display step counts. Capped daily totals are stored securely in your private cloud profile to sync your progress. It is not used for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to fitness tracking, and is never sold or shared with third parties.

You can manage or revoke the Activity Recognition / Physical Activity permission from your Android device settings.

Data Retention

We retain your account data only while your account is active or as needed to provide app features. When you permanently delete your account, associated cloud data is deleted or anonymized, except where limited retention is required for security, fraud prevention, purchase records, or legal compliance.

Photos and Camera Access

AURA-FIT may request camera or gallery access when you choose to upload a profile photo or progress photo. Uploaded images are stored in Cloudinary and linked to your account through Firestore URLs.

Notifications

AURA-FIT may send local notifications for workout reminders, fasting status, activity completion, and progress updates. You can disable notifications in your device settings or inside the app where available.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your data may be processed by the service providers listed above only to operate app features such as authentication, cloud sync, image hosting, premium purchases, and analytics shown to you inside the app.

Security

Data is transmitted securely using HTTPS. Firestore security rules are designed so authenticated users can access only their own user data under their Firebase UID.

Account Deletion

You can request account deletion inside AURA-FIT from Profile/Settings/Account & Security. Permanent deletion removes your account and associated cloud data, including profile data, workout logs, progress, premium status records, and saved image references.

You can also visit our dedicated account deletion page: Account deletion instructions.

Your Choices

  • You can update your profile information inside the app.
  • You can revoke camera, notification, or Physical Activity (pedometer) permissions from Android settings.
  • You can deactivate your account temporarily where supported by the app.
  • You can permanently delete your account and associated data.

Children's Privacy

AURA-FIT is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us so we can delete it.
