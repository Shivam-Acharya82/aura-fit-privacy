AURA-FIT Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026

AURA-FIT is a fitness tracking app that helps users manage workouts, progress photos, step/activity tracking, fasting logs, premium access, and fitness analytics.

Privacy, security, and user control

Information We Collect

Depending on the features you use, AURA-FIT may collect and process:

  • Account information such as name, email address, gender, age, and profile photo.
  • Authentication data required for email login and Google login.
  • Workout data such as completed exercises, workout logs, calories, and training progress.
  • Progress and memory data such as weight entries, progress photos, and transformation logs.
  • Premium/subscription data such as premium status, payment status, plan, and expiry date.
  • Health Connect data such as steps, distance, and active calories burned.
  • Location data during running sessions to calculate distance, pace, and speed.
  • Camera/gallery images selected by you for profile photos and memories.
  • Notification preferences for workout reminders, fasting, and activity completion.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your AURA-FIT account.
  • To sync your data across devices when you sign in with the same account.
  • To show workout progress, step reports, metabolic trends, and analytics.
  • To save progress photos and show transformation history.
  • To calculate activity stats such as distance, calories, duration, and running speed.
  • To manage premium access and verify purchases.
  • To send reminders and completion notifications when enabled.
  • To improve app reliability, security, and user experience.

Cloud Services We Use

  • Firebase Authentication: used for email login and Google login.
  • Firebase Firestore: used to store profile data, premium status, workout progress, logs, settings, and Cloudinary image URLs.
  • Cloudinary: used to upload and host profile photos, memory photos, and other user-selected images.
  • Google Play Billing: used for premium purchases on Android builds.

AURA-FIT does not store image files directly in Firestore. Firestore stores only the image URL returned by Cloudinary.

Health Connect and Fitness Data

If you grant Health Connect permission, AURA-FIT reads steps, distance, and active calories burned to power the step counter, progress reports, calorie burn totals, and fitness analytics.

You can manage or revoke Health Connect permissions from Android settings or the Health Connect app.

Location Data

AURA-FIT may request location permission for running sessions. Location is used to calculate running distance, real-time speed, pace, and activity logs. Location is used only while a running session is active.

Photos and Camera Access

AURA-FIT may request camera or gallery access when you choose to upload a profile photo or progress photo. Uploaded images are stored in Cloudinary and linked to your account through Firestore URLs.

Notifications

AURA-FIT may send local notifications for workout reminders, fasting status, activity completion, and progress updates. You can disable notifications in your device settings or inside the app where available.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your data may be processed by the service providers listed above only to operate app features such as authentication, cloud sync, image hosting, premium purchases, and analytics shown to you inside the app.

Security

Data is transmitted securely using HTTPS. Firestore security rules are designed so authenticated users can access only their own user data under their Firebase UID.

Account Deletion

You can request account deletion inside AURA-FIT from Profile/Settings/Account & Security. Permanent deletion removes your account and associated cloud data, including profile data, workout logs, progress, premium status records, and saved image references.

If you host a separate account deletion page, link it here: Account deletion instructions.

Your Choices

  • You can update your profile information inside the app.
  • You can revoke camera, location, notification, or Health Connect permissions from Android settings.
  • You can deactivate your account temporarily where supported by the app.
  • You can permanently delete your account and associated data.

Children's Privacy

AURA-FIT is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us so we can delete it.
