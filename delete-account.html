AURA-FIT Account Deletion

Last updated: May 24, 2026

This page explains how AURA-FIT users can deactivate their account temporarily or permanently delete their account and associated app data.

User-controlled account removal

Delete Your Account Inside the App

You can delete your account directly inside AURA-FIT:

  1. Open the AURA-FIT app.
  2. Sign in to the account you want to delete.
  3. Go to Profile.
  4. Open Settings or Account & Security.
  5. Tap Delete Account or Deactivate Account.
  6. Choose either temporary deactivation or permanent deletion.
  7. Confirm your choice in the warning dialog.

Temporary Deactivation

If you choose temporary deactivation, your account is disabled for login until you reactivate it. Your cloud data is kept safely so it can be restored when you reactivate your account.

Temporary deactivation may keep:

  • Profile data
  • Workout progress and logs
  • Memories and progress photo URLs
  • Premium status records
  • Settings and app preferences

Permanent Deletion

Permanent deletion removes your account and associated AURA-FIT cloud data. This action cannot be undone.

Permanent deletion is designed to remove:

  • Firebase Authentication account
  • Firestore profile data
  • Workout progress, workout logs, and activity logs
  • Premium/subscription status records stored by the app
  • Memory records and Cloudinary image URLs stored in Firestore
  • Settings, analytics, challenge data, body stats, and app preferences

AURA-FIT stores image files in Cloudinary and stores the returned image URLs in Firestore. The app removes the cloud data and image references associated with your account. If you need support removing externally hosted image assets, contact support using the email below.

Deletion Timeline

In-app deletion starts immediately after confirmation. Email deletion requests are reviewed and processed as soon as reasonably possible after account ownership is verified.
